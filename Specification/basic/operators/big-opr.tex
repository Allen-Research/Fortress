\section{Big Operators}
\seclabel{big-operators}

A big operator application is either a \emph{reduction expression}
described in \secref{reduction-expr} or a \emph{comprehension}
described in \secref{comprehensions}.



\Library\ define several big operators including $\sum$, $\prod$,
and \EXP{\OPR{BIG}\: \wedge}.

\See{big-operators-impl} for a discussion of how big operators are declared.
